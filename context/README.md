# context/

Who you are and how your business runs. `onboard` fills this from your intake.

Expected files (created during onboarding):
- `about-me.md` — your identity, role, and biggest time-sink
- `about-business.md` — what you sell, who you sell to, how revenue works
- `priorities.md` — your 90-day priorities
- `team.md` — your people (add when relevant)

Keep each note linked into the graph with `[[wikilinks]]` and a `## Connected` section.
